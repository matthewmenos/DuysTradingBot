// Arbitrage Engine

Spot the edge.
Get notified first.

DUYS scans two types of arbitrage every 3 minutes across all your connected exchanges. Each new opportunity fires an immediate Telegram alert — the same opportunity won't spam, but a different pair always notifies right away.

📊
Cross-Exchange — buy on the exchange with the lower ask, sell on the one with the higher bid. Net spread must exceed both taker fees plus a transfer cost buffer.
🔺
Triangular — exploit mispricing across three pairs on a single exchange. All three legs are simulated with compounding fee deductions before any alert is sent.
🪙
Token Picker — choose exactly which tokens to scan. Fewer tokens = faster scans. Change your selection anytime from the /arbitrage control panel.
